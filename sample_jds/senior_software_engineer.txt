Title: Senior Software Engineer - FinTech Platform

About Us:
We are a fast-growing FinTech startup building next-generation payment systems. We operate in a highly collaborative, fast-paced environment where software developers take extreme ownership of their products.

Role Description:
We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer to design, build, and maintain our high-throughput backend APIs. In this role, you will lead a small team of engineers, mentor junior developers, and drive architectural decisions. You will work independently to solve complex technical problems and must be comfortable making decisions with minimal guidance.

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
- 5+ years of hands-on experience building production backend systems.
- Strong proficiency in Python or Go.
- Proficient with SQL databases like PostgreSQL or MySQL.
- Extensive experience designing RESTful APIs.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with AWS (Amazon Web Services) cloud infrastructure.
- Familiarity with containerization tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
- Previous experience in the FinTech or payment processing domain is a huge plus.
- Adaptability and a strong willingness to learn new technologies as our stack evolves.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and optimize high-concurrency payment APIs.
- Mentor junior engineers and perform code reviews.
- Drive backend development and database schema designs.
- Troubleshoot production issues and improve system reliability.
